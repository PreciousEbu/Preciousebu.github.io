About me

A brief description

With over two years of expertise in the area, I can say I am a driven and committed Data Scientist.
Despite having a bachelor's degree in a non-computational subject, I was able to break into the tech field after receiving professional training and acquiring real-world experience through internships.
Currently, I work as a Data Scientist at a Software Tech company. Nonetheless, I am working towards obtaining my Master's degree in Data Science in the nearest future.

So far in my career, I have worked on numerous projects involving data cleaning, data analysis, data visualization, and machine learning. I am knowledgeable in programming languages such as Python and R, and I am familiar with SQL databases such as PostgreSQL and My SQL, creating Tableau dashboards and statistical analysis with SPSS

What I find so fascinating about data science is the ability to extrapolate insights and arrive at informed decisions based on data. In order to guarantee that I provide the most suitable answers to complex problems, I am consistently studying and keeping pace with the latest advancements in the industry.

I am excited about the opportunities that lie ahead and eager to apply my skills and knowledge to new challenges.

A Few Accomplishments

This section highlights some real-life problem solving projects that I have completed. In these projects, I applied major techniques associated with a data science project life cycle such as Data Cleaning, Data Preprocessing, Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), Data Visualization, Machine Learning and Predictive Modeling.

Credit Card Fraud Prediction in Python

In this project, I explored and analyzed a highly imbalanced dataset using Python and different machine learning algorithms to train and develop a classification model that predicts credit card fraud with high accuracy.

Telco Customer Churn Prediction with R

Revolutionizing the way telco companies retain their customers with advanced machine learning techniques. In this project, I developed a classification model, that predicts the likehood of customers to churn by analyzing extensive data on customer behavior and preferences, providing valuable insights into customer retention strategies.

Dashboard Visualization with Tableau

By leveraging on Tableau, I am able to showcase my expertise in creating interactive dashboards that allow users to explore and understand their data by exploring complex data sets and providing users with valuable insights at a glance.

Data Exploration with SQL

This project demonstrates my ability to manipulate large datasets, extract valuable information, and transform raw data from complex and relational databases using SQL.

Heart Disease Prediction in R

Here, I used R to explore and analyze the heart disease dataset and developed a classification model that predicts the occurence of heart disease when exposed to a combination of lifestyle features.

Web Scraping in Python

With the help of powerful Python libraries, I extracted valuable data from websites at scale in this project. By automating the data collection process, this project focuses on my ability to quickly gather and analyze large amounts of data from multiple disparate sources
